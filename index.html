Ornaments (Alankar)

Learn meend, khatka, murki and other vocal ornaments

Meend (मींड)

A smooth glide from one note to another, characteristic of Indian classical music.

Khatka (खटका)

Quick grace notes struck rapidly, adding brightness to a note.

Murki (मुर्की)

A cluster of notes sung rapidly, creating an ornamental phrase.

Andolan (आंदोलन)

A slow oscillation around a note, creating a gentle wavering effect.
